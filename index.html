INOK Document Scanner

Official Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 19, 2026

Our Core Philosophy: INOK Document Scanner is designed as an offline-first utility. All scan operations, optical text extraction (OCR), filter rendering, and PDF generations take place exclusively on your physical device. We do not transmit, sync, or host your confidential document data on external cloud servers.
1. Device Permissions & Usage

To deliver advanced document scanning, rotation, and file output services, the App requires standard hardware access permissions. These are managed locally:

  • Camera Access: Used solely to capture photos of physical papers to produce scanned documents.
  • Storage/Media Access: Used to import custom photos/files from your local gallery and to write your generated PDFs or JPEG files back to your device storage.
  • Network Access: Used occasionally to process local Wi-Fi print queues or to transmit anonymous background system crash report logs.
2. Integrated Third-Party Services

To maintain high application uptime, deliver fast bug fixes, and facilitate safe monetization paths, the App includes Google-approved service libraries. These may collect anonymous system descriptors:

  • Google Play Services: Runs core security and runtime libraries on Android.
  • Google AdMob (Optional/Monetization): Serves non-intrusive advertisements in the free version of the app.
  • Google Play Billing (Optional/Upgrades): Manages secured payment interfaces for premium license unlocks.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Records completely anonymous, non-personal runtime logs to isolate system errors.
3. Data Deletion & Ownership

Since all data belongs exclusively to you and resides solely on your local smartphone, INOK has no physical or digital access to your document files. Uninstalling the app via standard Android processes will erase the private local directory database. Backup and data preservation are entirely under the user's manual discretion.

4. Children's Privacy Shield

The App does not target, collect from, or market to children under the age of 13. If you believe we have accidentally received any information associated with a minor, please contact us immediately so we can purge the information from our logs.

5. Governing Law & Jurisdiction

This privacy policy is constructed, interpreted, and governed under the standard legal rules of India. Any resolution of dispute arising from the use of this software application shall be exclusively determined within the territorial court limits of India.

6. Policy Modifications

We reserve the right to modify this Policy periodically to align with evolving Android OS APIs and Play Store privacy updates. We encourage checking this URL regularly to stay updated with standard data policy guidelines.

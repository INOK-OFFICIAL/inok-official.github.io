INOK Document Scanner

Official Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 19, 2026

Our Core Privacy Commitment: INOK Document Scanner operates with absolute respect for user security. All image scaling, filters, cropping, optical text extraction (OCR), and PDF/JPEG outputs occur 100% locally on your smartphone. We never transmit or host your scanned folders, photos, or documents on external servers.
1. Device Permissions & Usage

To perform core physical-to-digital scanning processes, the application requests the following device permissions:

  • Camera Access: Necessary to snap pictures of physical documents to generate digital formats.
  • Storage/Media Access: Necessary to select files/images from your library for cropping and to save compiled PDFs locally.
  • Network Access: Necessary only to queue Wi-Fi document prints or to dispatch anonymous crash statistics.
2. Integrated Third-Party Services

We use standard Google-verified libraries for app stability and smooth performance. These SDKs may record fully anonymous system IDs:

  • Google Play Services: Ensures standard Android security policies.
  • Google AdMob (Optional): Displays harmless, relevant developer support ads inside the free tiers.
  • Google Play Billing (Optional): Encrypts transactions if purchasing a Premium features license.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Pinpoints app crashes anonymously to push out instant bug solutions.
3. Data Deletion & Storage

All scanned documents and databases are stored physically on your smartphone. We do not have any cloud portal or backend access to view or restore your files. Uninstalling the app completely will clear its private local database; please backup your documents manually before doing so.

4. Children's Privacy Shield

The App does not capture personal details or target users under 13 years of age. If you suspect any minor’s registration on our crash servers, notify us for immediate erasure.

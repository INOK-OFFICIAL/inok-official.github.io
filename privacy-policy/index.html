2. Localized "On-Device" Document Processing

INOK Document Scanner is fundamentally designed to operate as a local standalone utility. This means that all primary functionalities of the app are processed directly on your physical smartphone. No cloud servers are utilized to capture or parse your personal documentation:

  • Image Capture & Cropping: When you snap pictures of your physical receipts, bills, books, or papers, the scaling, rotating, and corner adjustment algorithms run entirely on your local CPU.
  • Document Enhancement & Filters: Adjusting contrast, applying grayscale filters, sharpening text, and brightness scales are processed on-device.
  • Optical Character Recognition (OCR): When you extract written text from your scanned files, the OCR engine analyzes the image layout and processes character recognition 100% offline on your smartphone. The recognized text never leaves your device.
  • PDF & JPEG Generation: Compiling multiple images into a final multi-page PDF document or high-quality JPEGs is performed locally using standard native library routines.
3. Device Permissions & Specific Usage Intent

To enable standard physical-to-digital scanning features, the application must interact with system hardware. We request only the absolute minimum required permissions as detailed below:

  • Camera Access (CAMERA): Required to activate the built-in device camera inside the app, allowing users to capture high-definition photographs of documents to initiate scanning.
  • Storage & Media Access (READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE): Required to import pre-existing images from your local device gallery for enhancements, and to write or save finalized compiled PDFs directly into your public storage/downloads folder.
  • Network Connectivity (ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE / INTERNET): Used solely to support background developer print queries (e.g., sending document files to local Wi-Fi print managers) and to transmit anonymous application telemetry as detailed below.
4. Integrated Third-Party Software & Analytics

To optimize stability, monitor layout crashes, and support ongoing developer updates, the Application integrates standard Google-verified software development kits (SDKs). These services may collect fully anonymized device metadata:

  • Google Play Services: Required to establish base security protocols, handle runtime environment layouts, and verify license configurations.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: In the event of an unexpected application crash, an anonymous, system-level trace log is compiled and transmitted to our development team. This report contains only device details (e.g., Android OS version, smartphone model, heap sizes) and contains absolutely no document text, filenames, or user information.
  • Google AdMob (Optional Tier): We may serve non-intrusive, relevant advertisements inside free versions of the application. AdMob may utilize unique advertising identifiers (AAID) to deliver personalized ads according to Google's standard ad policy.
  • Google Play Billing (Optional Purchase): For users choosing to unlock premium capabilities, billing transactions are handled entirely and securely by the encrypted Google Play Store billing portal. We never store or see your credit card, banking, or direct payment credentials.
5. Data Retention, Backup, & Purge Policy

Because INOK Document Scanner operates fully on-device without external server databases, your documentation is completely in your control:

Absolute Storage Limit: We do not back up, maintain, or synchronize your files in any remote cloud environment. Your scanned folders and saved documents remain securely confined within the private internal sandbox storage directories allocated to the App on your device.

Manual Backups Recommended: Users are solely responsible for exporting and backing up their valuable documents. If you decide to uninstall INOK Document Scanner or perform a factory reset on your smartphone, all locally cached images, on-device database lists, and settings will be permanently deleted and cannot be restored by our team.
